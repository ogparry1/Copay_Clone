The Secure, Shared Bitcoin Wallet

Secure your bitcoin with the open source, HD‑multisignature wallet from BitPay.

Get Ezpay

Secure, Shared Wallets

Ezpay is a true bitcoin wallet, not an account service. Secure your personal funds with multiple signatures, or just one.

Take security into your own hands and avoid trusting third parties with your savings. Like a joint-checking account, your Ezpay wallet can have multiple users. To send money, the group approves each transaction.

Manage shared funds with friends and coworkers. Share a wallet with your child and introduce them to bitcoin. Ezpay makes sharing a wallet simple and secure.

Multiple Wallets,
One App

No more tedious address management.

The Ezpay app securely stores multiple, distinct bitcoin wallets, allowing both business and privacy-conscious users to keep funds carefully separated.

Manage personal, business, and testnet wallets easily, all without leaving the app. Ezpay uses hierarchical-deterministic (HD) wallets, allowing for secure in-app wallet generation and backup.

Secure Payment Verification

Press that send button with confidence.

Ezpay is one of the first bitcoin wallets to support the full Bitcoin Payment Protocol (BIP 0070-0073).

When you send money to a Payment Protocol-enabled merchant, Ezpay securely verifies that your payment will go to the right place.

Testnet Support for Developers

The first bitcoin wallet to bring testnet support to both iOS and Windows Phone.

Ezpay includes native support for the Bitcoin Testnet, perfect for testing new bitcoin applications across multiple platforms.

With multisignature (P2SH) and multiple wallet support, Ezpay makes it simple for developers to test and demo bitcoin applications without fumbling between other mobile apps.

Open Source,
No Exceptions

Not only is the Ezpay app open source, the server is too.

Enterprise and power users can take full control of their finances by deploying their own version of the Ezpay apps and Bitcore Wallet Service.

Dozens of bitcoin developers have already contributed to Ezpay, and we're not finished yet. Every part of Ezpay, wallet and server, is available to be reviewed, improved, and developed by the community.

You can follow along, discuss improvements, and join in on GitHub at bitpay/ezpay and bitpay/bitcore-wallet-service.
